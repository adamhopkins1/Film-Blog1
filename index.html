The Power of the Dog wins the Golden Globe for Best Picture and Best Director at the 2022 Golden Globe Awards.

In A Season of Firsts Netflix recieves their First Major Award of the Season in the Runup to the Academy Awards in March, January 10th 2022.

Adam Hopkins,Content Writer

Jane Campion’s handsome, complex and brilliant western drama The Power of the Dog gets best picture (drama) and best director, along with best supporting actor for the excellent Kodi Smit-McPhee, while Steven Spielberg’s glorious, passionately respectful revival of West Side Story wins best picture in the musical or comedy section, along with its breakout player Ariana DeBose for best supporting actress and newcomer Rachel Zegler for best actress in musical/comedy.

Critics Choice Awards to continue in February According to the Critics Choice Associatiion

The Critics Choice Awards will continue in February 2022 at a unspecified date according to a statement released this morning.,Jan 9th 2022.

Adam Hopkins,Content Writer

The critics choice association and CW a subsideary of Warner Bros announced this morning via a statement that the 2022 Critics Choice Awards will be held on a later date in February 2022. The statement also indicated that per the Health Dept of California's order it will be decided prior to the event to be online only or in-person. In the state ment the Association which consists of over 4000 memebers of major guilds of critics and experts said "The decision to hold the Critics Choice Awards in February as opposed to the prior date of January was a unaminous decision made between the various guilds and the California Dept of Health due to the Peaking of the Omicron variant and based on models and timeline of decline we have decided in our best interest to hold the cermony at a later date in February 2022, To ensure the most minimal exposure for our public audiences and nomimnees." Currently the awards cermony which is to be presented in LA at the Beverly Hills Hotel was supposed to be originally held on January 9th 2022 but was postponed due to the continuing outbreaks of the Covid-19 Omicron variant. The CW said in a statement also that this decision "Will work in both our nominees and attendees best interests in accordance with the rules and guidance of the Calfornia Dept of Health".
